TM 55954 / LDAB 5669

Stable URI (with TM ID): www.trismegistos.org/text/55954

also known as Mertens-Pack 01869.000; P. Leyden V; Leyden Papyrus V; P. Leid. V; PLeid. V; PLeid.V.

Date: second half 3rd century AD: AD 250 - 299
cf. Johnson in Betz, Greek Papyri. Alternative date (Pack): AD 200 - 399; Van Haelst: AD 300 - 399

Provenance: Dios Polis (Thebes east) - Egypt (UThe region ca. 3rd cent. BC - AegyptusThe Roman provincia or regio ca. 2nd cent. AD) [found & written]

Language/script: Demotic / Greek / Hieratic / Old Coptic

continuation: four columns in Demotic, two with both Demotic and Greek, and thirteen in Greek. Several Old Coptic glosses, some Hieratic, vignettes

Material: papyrus

Recto/Verso: Vo

Reuse type: reuse of blank side of: TM 55946

Note: old LDAB 5671

More info: DCLP, MP3
