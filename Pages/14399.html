TM 14399

Stable URI (with TM ID): www.trismegistos.org/text/14399
Date: year 7 of Tiberius: AD 20 Aug 29 - 21 Aug 28
date mentioned (repayment): Tybi 28 in a subsequent year or later in the same year; cf. Pap. Congr. XIII (Marburg 1971) p. 212

Provenance: Tebtynis (Umm el-Baragat) - Egypt (00The region ca. 3rd cent. BC - AegyptusThe Roman provincia or regio ca. 2nd cent. AD) [found & written]

Language/script: Demotic / Greek / Latin

Greek subscriptions to a loan, Demotic signature, Greek note of registration and Greek acknowledgement of the return of the loan on the Ro; summary (title of the contract) in Greek and in Greek with Latin characters on the Vo

Material: papyrus

Recto/Verso: Ro/Vo, back: summary

Note: after repayment the loan was cancelled by a chi (X); on the fragment only the lower left-hand radius of this chi is evident, cf. Pap. Congr. XIII (Marburg 1971) p. 212.

More info: DDbDP (PN), HGV, PapPal
