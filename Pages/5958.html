TM 5958

Stable URI (with TM ID): www.trismegistos.org/text/5958
Date: year 9 of Ptolemaeus V or year 9 of Chaonnophris (South): BC 197 Oct 11 - 196 Oct 10

Provenance: Leontopolis (Tell el-Yahudiya), Nub Taha - Egypt (LThe region ca. 3rd cent. BC - AegyptusThe Roman provincia or regio ca. 2nd cent. AD) [found & written]

Language/script: Demotic / Greek / Hieroglyphic

the inscription was left unfinished and only part of the Greek is preserved; the parallel texts in the other languages have perhaps never been added

Material: stone (basalt) — stele

Note: duplicates: the Rosetta stone (TM 8809) & TM 6311

More info: PHI
