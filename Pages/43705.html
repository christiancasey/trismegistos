Date: BC 78 May 22

Provenance: Memphis - Egypt (LThe region ca. 3rd cent. BC - AegyptusThe Roman provincia or regio ca. 2nd cent. AD) [found & written]

Language/script: Demotic

Material: papyrus

Recto/Verso: Ro/Vo, back: witnesses

Reuse note: the right version (Pestman's II, Martin's 7b) is a first version in which an error has been made; the second, correct version is to the left (Pestman's I, Martin's 7a)
