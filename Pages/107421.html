TM 107421

Stable URI (with TM ID): www.trismegistos.org/text/107421
Date: BC 238 Mar 11
later than the Canopus Decree because of the mentions fifth phyle

Provenance: Tebtynis (Umm el-Baragat) - Egypt (00The region ca. 3rd cent. BC - AegyptusThe Roman provincia or regio ca. 2nd cent. AD) [found & written]

Language/script: Demotic

Material: papyrus

Recto/Verso: Ro/Vo, back: continuation

Reuse type: palimpsest new, effacing: see notes

Reuse note: palimpsest old: an earlier Demotic text but only a few signs are legible, probably a contract with a Greek docket

More info: BM

Publications

Journal of Egyptian Archaeology (JEA) 92 (2006), p. 209-212 (Monson, Andrew - 2006) (default)

Collections

London, British Museum EA 10647
