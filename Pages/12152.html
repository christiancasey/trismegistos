TM 12152

Stable URI (with TM ID): www.trismegistos.org/text/12152
Date: AD 41 Aug 24
P. Zauzich, p. 522, l. 8: 'Edgerton notes that Spiegelberg read the year as 1 but suggests that 2 should be read. The reading 1 does, however, look quite certain'. Both editions have wrong converted day: Aug 25 instead Aug 24; cf. ZPE 132 (2000), p. 224. In P. Zauzich 59 also the converted year is wrong: AD 042 instead 041.

Provenance: Tebtynis (Umm el-Baragat) - Egypt (00The region ca. 3rd cent. BC - AegyptusThe Roman provincia or regio ca. 2nd cent. AD) [found & written]

Language/script: Demotic

Material: papyrus

Recto/Verso: Ro, back: blank

More info: HGV
