Language/script: Demotic / Greek

Demotic contract with elaborate Greek subscription, Demotic autograph subscription and Greek note of registration; Greek contract with Greek subscription, Demotic autograph subscription and Greek note of registration

Material: papyrus

Recto/Verso: Ro, back: blank

Note: col. 1 has a Demotic sale and cession of 44 lines, followed by a Greek subscription for the seller Stotoetis by a Herieus; the buyer then provides a one-line subscription in Demotic in his own hand; col. 2 has a Greek loan, borrowed by Stotoetis from Apynchis; Herieus writes the Greek subscription for Stotoetis and Apynchis writes his own subscription in Demotic
