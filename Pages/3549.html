TM 3549

Stable URI (with TM ID): www.trismegistos.org/text/3549
Date: (a) BC 234 Aug 17 - Sep 15?
date of contract; date is based on registration (according to Egyptian calendar) and on eponymous priests (of year 14 of Macedonian calendar = BC 234 Jul 11 - 233 Jul 28 in P. L. Bat. 24)
(b) BC 234 Sep 5?
date of registration (according to Egyptian calendar); a date according to the financial calendar (BC 233 Sep 4) would contradict the information of the eponymous priests (if the conversion in P. L. Bat. 24 is coorect)
