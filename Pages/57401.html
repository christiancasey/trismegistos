TM 57401

Stable URI (with TM ID): www.trismegistos.org/text/57401

also known as P. Minutoli

previously also TM 56647 (double),

Date: BC 125 - 30?
alternative date: early Roman (Wild, in: BIFAO 72 (1972)); late Ptol. (Devauchelle, in: Enchoria 8.2 (1978))

Provenance: Egypt - Egypt (N/AThe region ca. 3rd cent. BC - AegyptusThe Roman provincia or regio ca. 2nd cent. AD) [found & written]

Language/script: Demotic / Hieratic

Hieratic text with the owner's name repeated in Demotic

Material: papyrus

Recto/Verso: Ro

Note: the join between the Paris and Geneva fragments was made by F. Herbin 1996 and confirmed by M. Etienne in 2008 (information courtesy Louvre)

More info: TBP
