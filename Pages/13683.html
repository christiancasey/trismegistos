TM 13683

Stable URI (with TM ID): www.trismegistos.org/text/13683
Date: (b) AD 64 Nov 29
date is that of repayment
(a) AD 64 Aug 1
the loan bears the same date as the registration; other date mentioned (loan to be repaid): year 10 of Nero Mesore 25 = AD 64 Aug 18

Provenance: Neilopolis (Tell el-Rusas) - Egypt (00The region ca. 3rd cent. BC - AegyptusThe Roman provincia or regio ca. 2nd cent. AD) [written]
Arsinoites (Fayum) - Egypt (00The region ca. 3rd cent. BC - AegyptusThe Roman provincia or regio ca. 2nd cent. AD) [found]

Language/script: Demotic / Greek

Greek contract with Demotic autograph subscription (borrower), Greek autograph subscription (lender) and Greek note of registration; Greek receipt for the repayment

Material: papyrus

Recto/Verso: Ro, back: blank

Note: after repayment the loan was cancelled by cross-lines

More info: DDbDP (PN), HGV, ÖNB
