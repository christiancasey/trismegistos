Stable URI (with TM ID): www.trismegistos.org/text/13693
Date: AD 143 Aug 30 - 144 May 25
Pauni (loan to be repaid) = AD 144 May 26 - Jun 24

Provenance: Soknopaiou Nesos (Dimeh) - Egypt (00The region ca. 3rd cent. BC - AegyptusThe Roman provincia or regio ca. 2nd cent. AD) [found & written]
cf. P. Vind. Worp, p. 106; P. Vind. Tand., p. 192

Language/script: Demotic / Greek

Greek contract (loan) with Demotic autograph subscription of the recipient Sochotes and Greek subscription written for him by a certain Soterichos

Material: papyrus

Recto/Verso: Ro/Vo, back: traces of a grapheion-stamp

Note: with red stamp; cf. P. Vind. Tand. 26 (same persons)

More info: DDbDP (PN), HGV, ÖNB
